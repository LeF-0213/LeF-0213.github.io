## Python
```python
import sys

n, m = map(int, sys.stdin.readline().split())
li = [0] * n

for _ in range(m):
    i, j, k = map(int, sys.stdin.readline().split())
    li[i - 1:j] = [k] * (j - i + 1)

print(' '.join(map(str, li)))
```
